# Privacy Policy

**Shala Shikshak**  
**Effective Date:** July 20, 2025

Shala Shikshak our respects your privacy. This app is designed to provide educational content (PDFs and YouTube videos) without collecting or storing any personal information from users.

## Information We Collect

We **do not collect**, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

The app:
- Displays publicly available and copyright-free PDFs in a WebView.
- Redirects users to educational videos hosted on YouTube.
- Does not require login or registration.
- Does not use cookies, analytics, or trackers.

## PDF and Video Content

All PDF files shown in the app are sourced from public domains or used with appropriate rights and are hosted securely.

YouTube links used in the app are publicly available and used solely for educational purposes.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect any user data, there is no risk of data misuse. The app does not connect to any external service other than to display web-hosted PDFs or open YouTube links.
