# Privacy Policy for VitTrack

**Last Updated: November 2, 2025**

## Introduction

VitTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application VitTrack (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Username
- Password (encrypted and securely stored)
- Profile information you choose to provide

### Financial Information
To provide expense tracking services, we collect:
- Transaction amounts
- Transaction descriptions
- Transaction categories
- Transaction dates
- Budget information

### Audio Recordings
When you use the voice input feature:
- We temporarily record your voice to process expense entries
- Audio recordings are sent to our AI service provider (Groq) for speech-to-text conversion
- Audio files are NOT permanently stored on our servers
- Audio data is processed in real-time and immediately deleted after transcription

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (type, operating system version)
- App usage statistics
- Error logs and crash reports
- Language preferences
- Currency preferences

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Process and categorize your expense transactions
- Generate analytics and insights about your spending
- Provide voice-to-text expense entry features
- Improve user experience and app performance
- Send you technical notices and support messages
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All transaction data is stored locally on your device
- Data is synchronized with our secure backend servers
- You can delete your local data at any time

### Backend Storage
- We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission (HTTPS)
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt encryption
- Our database is hosted on secure servers
- We implement regular security audits

### Data Retention
- Transaction data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can request deletion of your data at any time
- Audio recordings are deleted immediately after processing (not stored)

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Groq AI
- **Purpose**: Speech-to-text conversion and expense categorization
- **Data Shared**: Audio recordings (temporary), transaction descriptions
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://groq.com/privacy-policy/](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/)
- **Data Retention**: Audio is not stored; processed in real-time

### Expo
- **Purpose**: App development and deployment platform
- **Data Shared**: Anonymous usage statistics
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://expo.dev/privacy](https://expo.dev/privacy)

## Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

### Required Permissions
- **RECORD_AUDIO**: To enable voice input for expense entry
- **INTERNET**: To sync data with backend and process voice input
- **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**: To check internet connectivity

### Optional Permissions
- **NOTIFICATIONS** (future): To send budget alerts and reminders

You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Data Portability**: Export your transaction data
- **Withdraw Consent**: Opt-out of data collection (may limit functionality)

## Children's Privacy

VitTrack is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
- **With your consent**: When you explicitly agree to share data
- **Service providers**: Third-party services (like Groq AI) that help us operate the App
- **Legal compliance**: When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business transfers**: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## International Data Transfers
